Our Services

Providing tailored support to children, youths, and families in need.

Out of Home Care Services

We provide individualized and needs-based services for children and youths who are residing with carers. These include but are not limited to:

  • Alternative residence
  • Transitioning from care
  • Connection to essential services to allow children and youths to grow into their best selves

Respite: To allow carers to occasionally take a break from their crucial role, we provide respite services as regularly and as consistently as needed.

Family Contact Support

We believe in safe and positive time spent between children/youths and their families, especially when circumstances require children to live with carers for short- or long-term periods. Our services are tailor-made to meet each individual family’s circumstances, ensuring children and youths remain connected to their families.

Carer Assessments

We offer intra- and interstate carer assessment services to newly authorize or reauthorize carers based on their eligibility to look after the precious children who access our services. Our aim is to ensure that children and youths are cared for by the best possible individuals who are willing to make a difference, one life at a time.
