Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

Our phone line is open between 8am and 4:30pm CAT. Please give us a call should you need any more information.

You go through a carer assessment process which includes but is not limited to:
  • Submission of a Police Check
  • Working with Children’s Check
  • A medical report
One of our Assessors will come and spend time with you and your family as part of the process.

Simply complete a referral form or give our team a phone call during business hours.

Respite can be from one night to any approved number of nights. This will need to be discussed amongst the child/young person’s care team.
