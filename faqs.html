FAQs

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our services and processes.

How can I get more information?

Our phone line is open between 8am and 4:30pm CAT. Please give us a call should you need any more information.

How do I get assessed as a carer?

You go through a carer assessment process, which includes but is not limited to submission of a Police Check, Working with Children’s Check, and a medical report. One of our Assessors will come and spend time with you and your family as part of the process.

How can I get services for my family?

Simply complete a referral form or give our team a phone call during business hours.

How long can respite be for?

Respite can be from one night to any approved number of nights. This will need to be discussed amongst the child/young person’s care team.
