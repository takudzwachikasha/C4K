Our Story

C4K Child and Youth Services is a human services provider for children and youth in Australia. At C4K, we believe in ethical considerations in all we do. Our range of services are designed to optimise individual development. Our team of highly qualified, experienced and trained staff are just a phone call away to support families and professionals to streamline care provision.

Our journey began with a small group of dedicated individuals who believed that every child deserves a chance to thrive. Today, we continue to uphold that belief by offering a range of programs designed to nurture and empower the children in our care.
